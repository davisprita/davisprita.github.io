Tour Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Tel Aviv

Fly from any major U.A.E airport to Tel Aviv. Transfer to your hotel in Jerusalem. Welcome dinner in the evening.

Day2

Jerusalem - Old City

Walk the Via Dolorosa, visit the Church of the Holy Sepulchre and the Western Wall. Evening free to explore.

Day3

Jerusalem - Mt Olives & Gethsemane

Morning at the Garden of Gethsemane. Afternoon panoramic views from the Mt Olives.

Day4

Bethlehem

Day trip to Bethlehem. Visit the Church of the Nativity - the birthplace of Jesus Christ.

Day5

Dead Sea

Travel to the Dead Sea. Float on the saltiest body of water on earth, a truly unique experience.

Day6

Travel to Nazareth

Drive north to Nazareth. Check in to your hotel and explore the city where Jesus grew up.

Day7

Nazareth - Basilica & Old City

Visit the Basilica of the Annunciation and walk through Nazareth's ancient old city streets.

Day8

Sea Of Galilee

Take a boat ride on the Sea of Galilee. Visit Capernaum where Jesus performed many miracles.

Day9

Mt Beatitudes & Jordan River

Visit the MT Beatitudes where Jesus gave the Sermon on the Mt. Stop at the Jordan River

Day10

Free Day & Farewell Dinner

Spend the day exploring at your own pace. Farewell dinner in the evening.

Day11

Departure

Transfer to Tel Aviv airport. Return flight to the same major U.A.E airport. Your tour ends on arrival.
